Sound Design in Non-Traditional Instrument Contexts

Naomi Mason | 43565337

This report aims to investigate the role of sound design in the context of physical computing, particularly in the growing physical Musical Instrument Digital Interface (MIDI) controller sphere. In particular, this report will look at how this plays into the design of MozART; a large X/Y pad style instrument developed at the University of Queensland.
