MozART

A novel interface for musical expression.

Developed at The University of Queensland

MozART is an interactive instrument which produces interesting sounds and visuals responding to touches and pushes on a large spandex canvas. The project was developed by 4 undergraduate students from the ITEE faculty in Semester 1 of 2016, and has been developed further in conjunction with the Touch Ensemble in Semester 2 to be included as a part of this performance.
